Commercial Proposal

PulseFit – Digital Experience Platform
Prepared for: PulseFit Gym (India)
Prepared by: WebOrbs Studios
Date: January 24, 2026
Project Duration: 4–5 Weeks
Total Investment: ₹36,000 (GST Exclusive)

1. Executive Summary

This proposal outlines the development of PulseFit, a high-performance, full-stack gym management and digital experience platform.

The platform is engineered to streamline gym operations while providing a premium digital experience for members. It combines robust operational tools with a visually stunning, custom-built web interface.

2. Detailed Scope of Work & Feature Breakdown

2.1 Basic Platform Build (Foundation)

The core engineering and design required to bring the platform to life using Next.js 14 and Tailwind CSS.

  • UI/UX Design System: Custom layout, typography, color palette, and component library.
  • Public Website: Hero section, Services, About Us, and Facility showcase (Bento Grid).
  • Performance Engineering: SEO optimization, mobile responsiveness, and fast loading speeds.
  • Deployment Architecture: Secure hosting setup on Vercel with GitHub integration.

2.2 Admin Panel (Command Center)

A centralized dashboard for gym owners and staff to manage operations.

  • Member Management: Add/Remove members, view details, and manage status.
  • Staff Controls: Role-based access for trainers and receptionists.
  • Analytics Overview: Quick insights into new signups and active members.

2.3 Member Panel (User Dashboard)

A dedicated portal for gym members to manage their fitness journey.

  • Profile Management: Personal details and goal tracking.
  • Subscription Status: View active plans and renewal dates.
  • Attendance History: Log of past visits and workout frequency.

2.4 Payment Gateway Integration

Seamless, secure payment processing for memberships and services.

  • Integration with Razorpay/Stripe (UPI, Cards, Netbanking).
  • Automated receipt generation.
  • Secure transaction handling and status updates.

2.5 Real-Time Live Counter

An advanced feature to display real-time gym occupancy.

  • **"Pulse" Indicator:** Visual crowd meter (Low/Medium/High) on the website.
  • Live updates to help members plan their workout times.
  • Admin controls to override or calibrate counters.

2.6 Smart Attendance Management

Modern entry tracking system for security and analytics.

  • QR Code / Biometric Logic: Digital check-in logs.
  • Integration logic for physical scanner hardware (software side).
  • Daily/Weekly attendance report generation.
